Privacy Policy

Effective date: July 1, 2026

SupTrak ("we", "our", or "the app") is a personal health tracking tool. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle your information.

Data storage

All data you enter into SupTrak — including compounds, protocols, dose logs, health metrics, and meal logs — is stored locally on your device only. We do not collect, transmit, or store any of your personal health data on our servers.

What we do not collect

  • Personal identifying information (name, email, address)
  • Health or medical data
  • Location data
  • Usage analytics or tracking
  • Crash reports or diagnostics

Notifications

If you enable notifications, reminders are scheduled locally on your device using the browser Notifications API. No notification data is sent to any server.

Third-party services

SupTrak does not integrate with any third-party analytics, advertising, or data services. The app loads icon fonts from jsDelivr CDN for display purposes only — no personal data is shared.

Data deletion

You can permanently delete all your data at any time from Settings → Delete all my data. Uninstalling the app also removes all locally stored data from your device.

Children

SupTrak is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect information from children.

Medical disclaimer

SupTrak is a personal tracking tool only. It does not provide medical advice, recommend dosages, or endorse any substance. Always consult a licensed physician before starting any protocol.

Changes to this policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected with an updated effective date above.
